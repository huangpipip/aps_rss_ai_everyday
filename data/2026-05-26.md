  <summary>Details</summary>

Abstract: Multimodal optical characterization of Ni TeO identifies a uniform ferro-rotational state that couples polarity and chirality, leading to the formation of complex mixed-type domain walls.

<details>

Result: 观测到一种长寿命隐藏相，其特征为出现平衡态不存在的极化子和声子激发模式。

Abstract: Ultrafast photoexcitation in generates a long-lived hidden phase with unique polaronic and phononic excitations that do not exist under standard equilibrium conditions.

### [3] [Prethermalization, Shadowing Breakdown, and the Absence of Trotterization Transition in Quantum Circuits](https://link.aps.org/doi/10.1103/69d7-83xh)
*Marko Žnidarič*

Main category: prx

TL;DR: 利用截断传播子证明多体混沌系统中的阴影时间有限，并给出热化时间的计算方法，发现Trotter化相变本质上是有限尺寸效应。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探究多体混沌系统中阴影时间的有限性以及Trotter化相变的本质。

Method: 使用截断传播子理论进行分析。

Result: 阴影时间是有限的；热化时间可计算；Trotter化相变是有限尺寸效应。

Conclusion: 多体混沌系统的阴影时间非无限，且Trotter化相变随系统尺寸消失。

Abstract: New research uses truncated propagators to show that shadowing time in many-body chaotic systems is finite. It also offers a method to calculate thermalization time, finding that the “Trotterization transition” is generically a finite-size effect.

</details>


### [4] [Heating Dynamics of Mesoscopic Electron Baths at High Magnetic Field](https://link.aps.org/doi/10.1103/p63c-vpdv)
*F. Zanichelli,A. Veillon,C. Piquard,A. Aassime,Y. Sato,A. Cavanna,Y. Jin,J. Folk,U. Gennser,A. Anthore,F. Pierre*

Main category: prx

TL;DR: 通过时分辨噪声测温法，发现介观金属岛中存在由电子量子通道和核自旋耦合引起的两步热化过程。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索介观系统中电子与核自旋之间的热化机制，理解量子通道对能量弛豫的影响。

Method: 利用时间分辨噪声测温技术测量介观金属岛的电子温度动态。

Result: 观测到两步热化行为，表明热化过程分为快速电子-电子弛豫和慢速电子-核自旋能量交换两个阶段。

Conclusion: 电子量子通道与核自旋的相互作用是介观金属岛两步热化过程的主导机制，为理解量子热化提供了新视角。

Abstract: Time-resolved noise thermometry on mesoscopic metallic islands reveals a two-step thermalization process arising from the interplay between electronic quantum channels and nuclear spins.

</details>
