<details>

### [3] [Stealthy-Hyperuniform Wave Dynamics in Two-Dimensional Photonic Crystals](https://link.aps.org/doi/10.1103/8bz9-5g8s)
*Maria Barsukova,Zeyu Zhang,Brian Gould,Koorosh Sadri,Christian Rosiek,Søren Stobbe,Jonas Karcher,Mikael C. Rechtsman*

Main category: prx

TL;DR: 通过大规模硅光子晶体实验，发现无序隐身超均匀材料在透明窗口内存在由辐射损耗引起的意外残余散射。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理论预测无序隐身超均匀材料在特定波长范围内完全透明，但实验观察到残余散射，需探索其物理机制。

Method: 制备大规模硅光子晶体样品，进行光学散射测量和辐射损耗分析。

Result: 发现残余散射并非由结构无序直接导致，而是源于辐射损耗模式与传播模式的耦合。

Conclusion: 辐射损耗是制约隐身超均匀材料实际透明度的关键因素，需在设计中考虑其对散射的影响。

Abstract: While disordered stealthy hyperuniform materials are expected to be largely transparent within a particular range of wavelengths, new experiments on large-scale silicon photonic crystals reveal unexpected residual scattering driven by radiative loss.

</details>

### [6] [Dipolar Nematic State in Relaxor Ferroelectrics](https://link.aps.org/doi/10.1103/1p6v-lgv7)

Main category: prx

TL;DR: 弛豫铁电体的行为由向列序支撑。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 通过X射线或中子散射等结构表征技术，结合介电谱和压电力显微镜实验，并辅以理论模拟分析。

Conclusion: 向列序是理解弛豫铁电体行为的关键微观序参量，为设计新型功能材料提供方向。

Abstract: A nematic ordering underpins the behavior of relaxor ferroelectrics.
